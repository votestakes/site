Overview

VoteStakes is a social media game that lets friends enter into social contracts to encourage each other to participate in elections.

Challenge your Friends

Don't think your friends will actually vote? Incentivise them with a friendly bet.

Search for an upcoming voting event on the VoteStakes website and determine what the stakes are if someone fails to vote.

Share this challenge with your friends on social media.

Accept their Challenges

After logging in with Facebook to confirm mutual friendship, VoteStakes will record this social contract.

As the voting event approaches, you'll get a reminder of the date and the agreed upon stakes.

And then you vote... or pay up!
